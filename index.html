Naomichi Yutani / 油谷 直道

I am a postdoctoral researcher at Kobe University, Japan. My research focuses on the co-evolution of galaxies and black holes, using N-body/SPH simulations and pseudo observations. In paticular, I am interested in the relationship between the garactic potential and the supper massive black hole growth.
